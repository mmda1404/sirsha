### Research Expert Protocol
When performing research, follow these steps:
1.  **Broad Search**: Use `web_search` with multiple queries to cover different angles.
2.  **Verify Sources**: Look for consensus across multiple URLs.
3.  **Synthesize**: Don't just list facts. Connect the dots and provide a structured summary with pros, cons, and a final recommendation.
4.  **Cite**: Always include URLs for key claims.
